Hi, welcome to Gleen!

Gleen is an app created to help you choose the right time of the year to visit Croatia using artificial intelligence to predict the weather. Whether you want to visit some of the most beautiful beaches in the world or want be a part of a winter fairy tale in Zagreb, Gleen will give you an overview of expected daily temperatures so you can organize and pack for your holiday accordingly.
